## The Non-Negotiables

Before you spend a single credit, lock the **non-negotiables**.  
Once these are defined, build becomes mechanical.

### 1️⃣ Exact Scope Lock (No Feature Creep)

Your MVP must include only:

**Public Side:**
- Homepage  
- City page template  
- Event detail template  
- Safety + legal content hub  
- Postcode search  
- Email capture by postcode

**Organiser Side:**
- Account creation  
- Create event  
- Upload details + images  
- Free listing  
- Upgrade to Featured (£99–£199)  
- Stripe payment  
- Automatic expiry Nov 6  
- Automatic reminder next August

**Nothing else.**

No dashboards.  
No analytics.  
No ratings.  
No chatbots.  
No community features.

**If it's not on that list, it does not get built.**

This protects profit and timeline.

## 4️⃣ Database Structure (Season-Proof)

Before coding, you must define:

**Tables:**
- users
- events
- payments
- cities
- email_subscribers

**Events must include:**
- featured (boolean)
- expires_at (datetime)
- status (active/expired)
- year (2026, 2027, etc.)

**If this is wrong now, you rebuild in Year 2.**

We avoid that.
